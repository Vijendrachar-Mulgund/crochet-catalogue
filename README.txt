CHARMING YARNS — CATALOGUE
==========================

A simple, private catalogue for Charming Yarns crochet products.

TWO WAYS TO RUN IT
------------------
1. ON THIS COMPUTER ONLY (default) — runs offline, data saved in this
   browser. Great for getting started. See "HOW TO OPEN" below.

2. SHARED ONLINE (you + your mother together) — a real website with a login,
   so you both add photos to ONE catalogue from any computer. Free to set up.
   To switch to this mode, follow:
     • SUPABASE_SETUP.md   (create the shared database + your login)
     • DEPLOY.md           (publish the website and get a link to share)
   Once you paste your keys into js/config.js, the app automatically becomes
   the shared online version.

HOW TO OPEN
-----------
Recommended: double-click "Open Catalogue.command". It opens the catalogue
in your web browser and makes sure your data saves reliably. (The first time,
macOS may ask you to confirm opening it — right-click it and choose "Open".)
You can close the small Terminal window once the browser has opened.

Alternative: you can also just double-click "index.html" to open it directly.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
---------------
• Settings  – Add your business name, tagline, and logo. These appear on the
              cover of every PDF you share.
• Catalogue – Add products with a photo, price, category, materials, size,
              colours, and a "made to order" option. Search and filter them.
• Categories– Create and reorder groups (e.g. Toys, Bags, Home & Decor).
• Create a Share – This is for sending to customers:
     1. Tick the products and/or whole categories you want to include.
     2. Choose whether to "Show prices" (turn it OFF to quote later).
     3. Add an optional title (like the customer's name).
     4. Click "Preview & Save as PDF". In the print window that opens,
        choose "Save as PDF" as the destination, then send that file on
        WhatsApp or email.

IMPORTANT — BACKUPS
-------------------
Your catalogue is stored inside this browser on this computer. To keep it
safe (and to move it to another computer), go to Settings → "Export backup".
This saves a single file. Keep it somewhere safe (e.g. Google Drive).
To restore, use Settings → "Restore from backup".

Tip: export a fresh backup whenever you've added several new products.
